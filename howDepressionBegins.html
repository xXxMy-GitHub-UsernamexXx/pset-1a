Causes of Depression

Loneliness

One way depression begins to arise in a person is continued loneliness. This can be purposeful and accidental. It can be purposefully done by the depressed person by actively trying to isolate themself from the outside world; those in places of authority can also enforce such policies on others knowingly or not. This can also happen accidentially by unknowingly pushing people away. This leads to a neverending cycle of further isolation and stronger feelings of loneliness, leading the person to have a depressive mental state.

Emotional Abuse

Depression can also stem from prolonged emotional abuse. One common form of emotional abuse is bullying, the belittling of a person by another or one's self.
